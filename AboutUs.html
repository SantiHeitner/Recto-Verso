About Recto-Verso

Recto-Verso is Lycée Rochambeau's literary magazine. Recto-Verso is a tool for all students throughout the school to have their writing or works of art published. We release a new edition twice a year. Our first edition comprises of content sent to us before Thanksgiving and our second from before the April vacation. Each publication has a theme that is set to guide you in a direction but not limit your creativity. Texts not relating to the theme are perfectly fine. Images add color and complexity to the magazine and help us make each publication more stunning than the last. All of these components put together compose a magazine for the students and by the students.



Make sure to send us your creations,

-The Recto-Verso team
